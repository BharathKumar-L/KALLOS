Our Product Line

1. UniFlick Core Platform

The backbone of our solution, UniFlick Core, offers seamless and secure payment processing. Designed specifically for educational institutions, it integrates effortlessly with your existing systems and enhances the overall payment experience for students and staff.

  • Offline Payment Capability
  • Customizable White-Label Solution
  • Enhanced Security
  • Real-Time Transaction Monitoring

2. UniFlick Mobile App

Our user-friendly mobile app allows students and staff to manage payments, view transaction history, and receive notifications—all from their smartphones. It’s a convenient tool that brings the full power of UniFlick to their fingertips.

  • Easy Payment Management
  • Push Notifications for Transactions
  • Customizable User Interface
  • Multi-Language Support

3. UniFlick Admin Dashboard

The Admin Dashboard is a powerful tool for institutional administrators, offering full control over payment processing, user management, and analytics. It’s designed to provide the insights and flexibility you need to manage your institution’s financial operations efficiently.

  • Detailed Reporting and Analytics
  • User and Role Management
  • Customizable Settings
  • Integration with Campus Systems

4. UniFlick Point-of-Sale (POS) Terminals

Our POS terminals are designed for easy installation in cafeterias, bookstores, and other campus facilities. They work seamlessly with the UniFlick system, ensuring quick and secure transactions in all campus locations.

  • Tap-and-Go Payment
  • Offline Transaction Processing
  • Durable and Easy to Use
  • Customizable Interface
